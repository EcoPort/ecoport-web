About EcoPort

EcoPort is a Bali-based plastic recycling and export company focused on collecting, cleaning, and shipping post-consumer HDPE plastic to global buyers. We aim to reduce waste, support local communities, and create sustainable value from plastic recovery.

Our Mission

Our mission is to clean up plastic waste in Bali by creating a seamless supply chain from collection to export. We partner with local businesses and communities to ensure that plastics are not only removed from the environment but reused in a meaningful way.
