About Us

EcoPort is a high-impact sustainability venture headquartered in Bali. Our mission is to collect, clean, and export HDPE plastic waste, connecting eco-conscious resorts, beach clubs, and local businesses with global recyclers. With a strong focus on operational transparency and environmental integrity, we're helping clean up the world—one shipment at a time.

Our Mission

To partner with businesses worldwide in building a scalable and ethical recycling network that not only transforms waste into resources but generates global environmental value.

Work With Us

If you're a resort, beach club, or corporate entity looking to boost your sustainability profile, EcoPort is here to help. We provide collection bins, streamlined logistics, and international compliance—so your waste becomes opportunity, not landfill.
